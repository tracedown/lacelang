# Governance

## Current model

Lace is currently being maintained by Tracedown contributors. Final decisions about the spec,
grammar, schemas, conformance suite, and bundled extensions are made by
the maintainers.

All meaningful contributions from the community are welcome and will be
integrated on merit. There is no distinction between "core" and
"external" contributors — the quality and correctness of the
contribution is what matters.

## Decision process

- **Spec changes**: discussed in GitHub issues, discussions, or PR
  comments. The maintainers make the final call.
- **New executor implementations**: reviewed by maintainers for
  conformance. Passing the testkit is the bar for listing.
- **Extensions**: bundled default extensions (`laceNotifications`,
  `laceBaseline`) are maintained alongside the spec. Community
  extensions live in their own repositories.

## Future

The goal is to move Lace to an independent foundation when adoption is
meaningful enough to warrant shared governance. Until then, Tracedown
stewards the project with the commitment to keep it open, neutral, and
welcoming to independent implementations.
